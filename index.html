Welcome

I am Junpei Zhong (Joni), a research associate (fulltime PhD) at University of Hamburg, under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Stefan Wermter. My work is also involved in and sponsored by the RobotDoc Collegium, an EU FP7 Marie Curie Initial Training Networks (ITN) on developmental robotics.

Brief Intro

I work in the area in biologically-inspired AI, and its application to cognitive robotics development. My current research encompasses the following interrelated research themes: Computational Neuroscience, particularly focusing on the visual system modelling, and its relation into the affordance learning in a mirror neuron system.

★ Bootstrap Learning for Visual Perception on Robots

★ Developmental Psychology and its contributions to Developmental Robotics

My previous research also includes SLAM, and its biological-inspired solutions.
